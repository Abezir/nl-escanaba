Statement of Faith
Aaron Broberg
God

I believe in one God — Father, Son, and Holy Spirit — eternally existing in three persons, the Author of all creation and the source of all life.

Jesus Christ

I believe in the Lord Jesus Christ, the eternal Son of God, who became flesh, lived a sinless life, died on the cross as full payment for the sins of all humanity, rose bodily from the dead on the third day, and ascended to the right hand of the Father. He is the only way to salvation, and I have built my life on that truth.

Scripture

I believe in the Holy Scriptures as the inspired, authoritative Word of God — the final authority for faith, doctrine, and daily life. It is not merely a book of knowledge; it is living instruction meant to be applied, not just studied.

The Holy Spirit

I believe in the Holy Spirit — the third person of the Trinity — who indwells every believer at salvation, empowers us for witness and service, and distributes spiritual gifts for the building up of the Church. I believe in the baptism of the Holy Spirit with the evidence of speaking in tongues, and I have experienced this in my own life and ministry.

The Church

I believe the Church is the Body of Christ — God's primary vehicle for His mission on earth. A healthy local church functions in five essential purposes:

  • Worship — bringing glory to God in all we do
  • Fellowship — building authentic community where people are known and loved
  • Discipleship — forming believers into the image of Christ
  • Ministry — equipping and releasing every person to serve
  • Evangelism — carrying the Gospel beyond our walls into our communities and the world

I believe a church that neglects any one of these five is not yet fully the Church. I have built my ministry around all five, and I have given my life to seeing them function together.

The Return of Christ

I believe in the imminent return of Jesus Christ, the resurrection of the dead, and the final judgment — and I live and minister with that eternal perspective in view.
